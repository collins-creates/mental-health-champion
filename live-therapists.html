Frequently Asked Questions

Are the therapists licensed?

Yes, all therapists on our platform are licensed mental health professionals with verified credentials. We conduct thorough background checks and license verification for every therapist.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are encrypted and completely confidential. We follow HIPAA guidelines and strict privacy protocols to protect your personal information and therapy sessions.

What technology do I need?

You need a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone), a stable internet connection, and a modern web browser. No software download is required.

Can I use insurance?

Many of our therapists accept insurance. You can check with individual therapists about their insurance policies. We also provide receipts for potential reimbursement.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel or reschedule sessions up to 24 hours before the appointment time without penalty. Late cancellations may be subject to the therapist's cancellation policy.

How do I choose the right therapist?

You can filter therapists by specialty, availability, language, and session type. Read their profiles, specialties, and approaches. Many therapists offer brief introductory consultations.
